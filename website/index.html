Questions

Frequently asked

What is Ritham?

Ritham is a Vedic astrology app that computes your birth chart (Kundli) and gives you daily horoscopes, an AI astrologer you can chat or talk to, panchang and muhurat, palm reading, numerology, a dream oracle, puja booking and detailed reports — all generated from your birth details.

How do I sign in?

Tap Continue with Google and choose your account — you're in instantly, with no passwords to remember.

How much does it cost?

Ritham is free to install and explore. AI chat packs, voice-call packs, live sessions and detailed reports are simple one-time purchases in Indian Rupees, processed securely by Razorpay. The exact price is always shown before you pay — see the Pricing section above.

Can I get a refund?

Because readings and reports are generated and delivered digitally the moment you buy, they are generally non-refundable. If you were charged but did not receive what you paid for, contact us and we'll make it right. Full details are in our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your birth details, chats and reports are visible only to you, and your payment details are handled by Razorpay and never stored by us. You can delete your account and all its data at any time from Settings → Delete Account.

Is astrology on Ritham a substitute for professional advice?

No. Readings are offered for guidance, reflection and entertainment, and are not a substitute for professional medical, legal, financial or psychological advice. Please see our Disclaimer.
